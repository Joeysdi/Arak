🏗 What to Look For in an Ark Property

Water
Spring, well, or river on-site

Municipal water unacceptable. Minimum 10,000L/day flow for 25-person community.

Solar Aspect
South-facing slope ≥45°

For Northern Hemisphere: south-facing. SH: north-facing. Minimum 6h direct sun.

Arable Land
≥2 acres per 10 residents

Caloric self-sufficiency requires ~0.5 acres intensive growing per person.

Access Control
Single entry road choke point

One defendable road in = one road to monitor. Natural barriers (river, cliff) superior.

Altitude
600–2,000m ideal

Above flood zones, below altitude sickness threshold. Natural climate regulation.

Forest Cover
≥30% woodland on property

Timber, firewood, aerial camouflage, wildlife habitat, soil health.
