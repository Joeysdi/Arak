Safe Zone Doctrine · Immigration Layer
L1
Primary Residency
Your current or primary legal home. May be the riskiest location by threat mapping. Foundation to build from, not rely on.
// LAYER ONE — ORIGIN
L2
Staged Bugout Location
Secondary property or rental within 4–6 hour drive. Legal right to reside. Low-profile. Pre-stocked. First refuge in rapid-onset scenarios.
// LAYER TWO — DOMESTIC
L3
Second Residency (Foreign)
Legal right to live in a Tier A or B nation. Fully documented. Property or long-term rental pre-arranged. Banking active. Family can arrive within 48h of flight booking.
// LAYER THREE — FOREIGN REFUGE
L4
Second Citizenship / Passport
Legal citizenship in a second nation. Highest-tier freedom asset. Allows entry without sponsorship. Can be passed to children. Permanent status unaffected by political changes in home country.
// LAYER FOUR — IDENTITY FREEDOM
L5
Third Jurisdiction Banking
Assets held in a neutral third jurisdiction unlinked to either citizenship. Accessible globally. Protected from asset freezes, sanctions, or capital controls in any single nation.
// LAYER FIVE — FINANCIAL SOVEREIGNTY
L6
Community Integration
Embedded in Ark-aligned network at foreign location. Not just a property owner — a known, trusted member of a local resilience community. Social capital is the ultimate non-confiscatable asset.
