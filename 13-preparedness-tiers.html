Investment Principle

Preparedness investment should follow the 80/20 rule: 80% of your resilience comes from the first 20% of spending. Tier 0 → 1 costs as little as $500 and eliminates the most acute risks. Never skip tiers to buy expensive gear — build the foundation first.
