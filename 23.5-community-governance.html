MODULE 23.5 — COMMUNITY SYSTEMS

Community Governance
& Vetting

The single most underestimated risk in any Ark strategy is internal collapse — community fractures, governance failures, and compromised members. This module establishes the complete Ark Governance Rulebook, Policy Matrix, and Vetting Protocol.

🏛️
Community Council
5-member elected body. Quorum required for major decisions. 2-year rotating terms. No single-family majority.
⚖️
Arbitration Panel
3 rotating mediators drawn from non-involved members. Conflict resolution within 72hrs. Binding in crisis.
🔐
Security Committee
Separate from Council. Controls access protocols, threat assessment, and emergency powers activation.
📋
Skills Registry
Updated quarterly. Tracks all medical, technical, agricultural, and security competencies across community.
🌾
Resource Committee
Food, water, energy, and medical inventory management. Rationing authority in declared emergencies.
📚
Education Collective
Manages knowledge transfer, children's education, skills training, and succession readiness programs.
CANDIDATE VETTING PROTOCOL

Assess a Candidate for Ark Membership

The Ark Vetting Protocol scores candidates across 7 dimensions. No single factor disqualifies — but pattern failures across multiple dimensions trigger a Community Council review or rejection.

THE ARK COMMUNITY RULEBOOK — v3

Core Governance Rules

These rules form the legal foundation of any Ark community. All members sign a Community Covenant on admission. Violations are categorised as Level 1 (warning), Level 2 (probation), or Level 3 (expulsion).

CONFLICT RESOLUTION PROTOCOLS

Structured Conflict Management

Historical evidence from intentional communities shows that conflict resolution failure is the #1 cause of community dissolution. The Ark Protocol codifies four escalating response tiers.

EMERGENCY POWERS DOCTRINE

Crisis Governance Framework

Normal governance becomes a liability in acute collapse scenarios. The Emergency Powers Doctrine grants temporary command-and-control authority under defined trigger conditions, with mandatory sunset clauses.
